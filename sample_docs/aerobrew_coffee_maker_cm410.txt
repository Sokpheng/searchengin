AeroBrew Coffee Maker - User Manual
Model: CM-410
Category: Kitchen
MSRP: $79

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 12-cup carafe
- 1000W heating element
- programmable 24-hour timer
- auto shutoff after 2 hours

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Rinse the carafe and filter basket before first use.
2. Fill the reservoir with cold water up to the 12-cup line.
3. Insert a paper filter or the reusable mesh filter into the basket.
4. Add ground coffee (1 tbsp per cup is recommended) and close the lid.
5. Press the Power button, then Brew to start.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E1: Water reservoir is empty or not seated correctly.
- E2: Heating element overheated; unplug and let cool for 30 minutes.
- E4: Carafe not detected on the warming plate.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (CM-410) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Do not operate with a cracked or chipped carafe.
- Unplug before cleaning; never immerse the base in water.
- Keep the steam vent clear of obstructions while brewing.

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 2-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
Extended to 3 years if registered online within 30 days of purchase.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Descale every 60 brew cycles using a 1:1 water-vinegar solution.
- Wash the mesh filter after every use.
- Wipe the warming plate with a damp cloth weekly.
