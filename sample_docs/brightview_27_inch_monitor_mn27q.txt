BrightView 27-inch Monitor - User Manual
Model: MN-27Q
Category: Electronics
MSRP: $249

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 2560x1440 resolution
- 165Hz refresh rate
- 1ms response time
- HDR400 support

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Attach the stand to the monitor base before mounting to the panel.
2. Connect via DisplayPort for full 165Hz refresh rate (HDMI caps at 144Hz).
3. Use the joystick on the rear panel to access the on-screen menu.
4. Enable HDR in your operating system's display settings.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E1: No signal detected; check cable connection and input source.
- E2: Refresh rate not supported by current cable; switch to DisplayPort.
- E5: Panel self-test failure; contact support.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (MN-27Q) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Do not press directly on the LCD panel.
- Use a surge protector; power spikes can damage the internal board.
- Allow ventilation space above and behind the monitor.

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 3-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
3-year warranty covering dead pixels beyond the industry-standard threshold.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Dust the vents on the rear panel monthly.
- Use a microfiber cloth only; avoid ammonia-based cleaners on the screen.
- Recalibrate color settings every few months for accuracy.
