ChopMaster Food Processor - User Manual
Model: FP-260
Category: Kitchen
MSRP: $149

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 9-cup work bowl
- 3 interchangeable blades
- 700W motor
- pulse and continuous modes

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Wash all detachable parts before first use.
2. Attach the work bowl to the base and lock the lid.
3. Insert the desired blade attachment onto the drive shaft.
4. Use the pulse button for chopping, continuous mode for purees.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E1: Lid not locked; the motor will not start.
- E3: Bowl overfilled past the max fill line.
- E4: Motor thermal cutoff triggered; wait 15 minutes before restarting.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (FP-260) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Handle blades by the plastic hub, never the cutting edge.
- Do not process ingredients hotter than 175°F.
- Unplug before disassembling the blade attachment.

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 2-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
2-year limited warranty on the motor base; blades are not covered.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Hand wash blades immediately after use to prevent dulling.
- Check the lid gasket for cracks every few months.
- Store blades in the included protective case.
