CleanCycle Washing Machine - User Manual
Model: WM-800
Category: Home
MSRP: $649

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 4.5 cu ft capacity
- 12 wash cycles
- 1200 RPM max spin speed
- ENERGY STAR certified

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Remove shipping bolts from the rear panel before first use.
2. Level the machine using the adjustable feet; check with a bubble level.
3. Connect hot and cold water hoses to the correct valves.
4. Run an empty calibration cycle before washing your first load.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E1: Door not latched; the machine will not start a cycle.
- E2: Water supply valve closed or hose kinked.
- E4: Unbalanced load detected during spin cycle.
- E8: Drain hose blocked or improperly positioned.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (WM-800) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Leaving shipping bolts installed can cause serious vibration damage; remove before use.
- Do not overload past the max fill line for your selected cycle.
- Turn off the water supply if leaving home for an extended period.

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 3-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
3-year warranty; drum and motor covered for 10 years under normal residential use.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Run a monthly cleaning cycle with washing machine cleaner.
- Leave the door ajar between uses to prevent mold and odor.
- Clean the detergent drawer every few weeks.
