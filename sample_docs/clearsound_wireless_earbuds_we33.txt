ClearSound Wireless Earbuds - User Manual
Model: WE-33
Category: Electronics
MSRP: $59

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- Bluetooth 5.3
- 24-hour total battery with case
- active noise cancellation
- IPX4 sweat resistance

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Charge the case fully via USB-C before first use (about 90 minutes).
2. Remove both earbuds from the case to enter pairing mode automatically.
3. Select the earbuds from your device's Bluetooth menu.
4. Use the companion app to customize the touch controls.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E1: Left and right earbuds out of sync; return both to the case for 10 seconds.
- E2: Case battery below 5%; charging required before use.
- E3: Firmware update failed; keep earbuds within 3 feet of your phone and retry.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (WE-33) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Do not submerge in water; IPX4 only protects against sweat and light rain.
- Keep volume below 85 decibels for extended listening sessions.
- Discontinue use if you experience ear irritation.

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 1-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
1-year warranty; excludes damage from moisture beyond IPX4 rating.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Clean the ear tips weekly with a dry, soft cloth.
- Avoid leaving the case in direct sunlight or hot cars.
- Replace ear tips every 6 months for the best seal.
