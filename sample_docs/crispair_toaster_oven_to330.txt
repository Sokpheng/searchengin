CrispAir Toaster Oven - User Manual
Model: TO-330
Category: Kitchen
MSRP: $99

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 0.6 cu ft interior
- 6 cooking functions
- 60-minute timer with auto shutoff
- fits a 9-inch pizza

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Remove all packaging materials from inside the oven.
2. Run the oven empty at 400°F for 10 minutes to burn off manufacturing residue.
3. Select a cooking function using the dial.
4. Set temperature and time, then press Start.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E1: Door not fully closed.
- E2: Internal temperature sensor fault.
- E7: Fan motor obstruction detected.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (TO-330) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Exterior surfaces become hot during use; use oven mitts.
- Do not line the crumb tray with foil.
- Keep at least 4 inches of clearance from walls and cabinets.

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 2-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
Standard 2-year warranty; heating elements covered for 3 years.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Empty the crumb tray after every 3-4 uses.
- Wipe the interior with a damp cloth once cool.
- Check the door seal yearly for gaps.
