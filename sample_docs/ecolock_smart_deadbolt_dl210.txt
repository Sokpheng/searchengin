EcoLock Smart Deadbolt - User Manual
Model: DL-210
Category: Smart Home
MSRP: $179

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- fingerprint, code, and app unlock
- auto-lock after 30 seconds
- battery backup (4 AA, 6-month life)
- fits standard door prep

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Verify your door thickness (1-3/8 to 2 inches) matches compatibility before installing.
2. Remove the existing deadbolt and retain mounting screws if compatible.
3. Install the exterior and interior assemblies, connecting the wiring harness between them.
4. Insert batteries and complete setup through the companion app.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E1: Low battery warning; replace all 4 AA batteries as a set.
- E2: Fingerprint not recognized after 3 attempts; re-enroll in the app.
- E4: Door misaligned; deadbolt cannot extend fully into the strike plate.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (DL-210) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Keep a physical backup key accessible in case of full battery failure.
- Do not use rechargeable batteries; voltage drop can cause malfunctions.
- Test the lock thoroughly before relying on it as your primary door lock.

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 2-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
2-year warranty on electronics; mechanical deadbolt components covered for 5 years.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Replace all 4 batteries together every 6 months, not individually.
- Lubricate the bolt mechanism yearly with graphite lubricant.
- Wipe the fingerprint sensor with a dry cloth if recognition slows down.
