EmberGlow Camp Stove - User Manual
Model: CS-100
Category: Outdoor
MSRP: $69

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 2-burner design
- 10,000 BTU per burner
- piezo ignition
- folds to 16x4x14 inches

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Attach the propane canister to the regulator hose before igniting.
2. Check all connections for gas leaks using soapy water before first use.
3. Turn the burner knob and press the piezo igniter simultaneously.
4. Allow the stove to cool completely before folding for storage.

3. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Never use inside a tent or enclosed space; carbon monoxide risk.
- Keep at least 24 inches from flammable materials.
- Check for gas leaks before every use, not just the first.

4. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 2-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
2-year warranty on burner components; regulator hose covered for 1 year.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

5. MAINTENANCE
- Clean burner ports with a pin if flame is uneven.
- Store the propane canister separately in a cool, dry place.
- Inspect the hose for cracks each season.
