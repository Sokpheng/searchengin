FreshBrew Electric Kettle - User Manual
Model: EK-170
Category: Kitchen
MSRP: $44

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 1.7L capacity
- 1500W rapid boil
- 5 temperature presets
- keep-warm mode for 30 minutes

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Rinse the interior with water before first use; discard the first boil.
2. Fill to the desired level between the min and max lines.
3. Select a temperature preset for tea, coffee, or general boiling.
4. Press the keep-warm button if you want water held at temperature.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E1: Kettle not seated correctly on the power base.
- E2: Dry-boil protection triggered; add water before continuing.
- E4: Temperature sensor fault; contact support if error persists.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (EK-170) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Do not fill above the max line to avoid boiling water splashing out.
- Do not operate without the lid closed.
- Descale regularly to prevent heating element damage.

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 1-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
1-year warranty; excludes mineral scale buildup damage.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Descale monthly in hard water areas using a vinegar solution.
- Wipe the exterior with a soft, dry cloth.
- Check the power base contacts for moisture before each use.
