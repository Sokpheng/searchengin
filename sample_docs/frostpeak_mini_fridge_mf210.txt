FrostPeak Mini Fridge - User Manual
Model: MF-210
Category: Kitchen
MSRP: $189

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 3.2 cu ft capacity
- reversible door
- adjustable thermostat (34-45°F)
- quiet compressor (under 40dB)

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Let the unit stand upright for 4 hours before plugging in.
2. Place on a level, ventilated surface with 3 inches of clearance on all sides.
3. Set the thermostat dial to level 3 for normal use.
4. Allow 24 hours to reach set temperature before loading with food.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E1: Door left open for more than 5 minutes.
- E3: Compressor fault; contact support if error persists after restart.
- E5: Ambient temperature too high for normal operation (above 100°F).
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (MF-210) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Do not block the rear ventilation grille.
- Do not store flammable items or aerosol cans inside.
- Disconnect power before moving the unit.

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 1-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
Compressor covered separately for 5 years; all other parts 1 year.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Defrost manually every 3-6 months if frost exceeds 1/4 inch.
- Clean the door gasket monthly with mild soap.
- Vacuum the rear coils twice a year.
