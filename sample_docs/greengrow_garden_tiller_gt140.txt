GreenGrow Garden Tiller - User Manual
Model: GT-140
Category: Outdoor
MSRP: $219

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 40V battery powered
- 10-inch tilling width
- 6-inch tilling depth
- foldable handle for storage

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Charge the battery fully before first use (approx. 2 hours).
2. Attach the tines by aligning the mounting holes and inserting the pins.
3. Adjust the handle height to a comfortable working position.
4. Start on a low-depth setting for compacted or unfamiliar soil.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E1: Battery not seated correctly in the compartment.
- E3: Tines jammed with roots or debris; power off before clearing.
- E5: Motor overload; reduce tilling depth and retry.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (GT-140) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Wear sturdy closed-toe shoes and eye protection while operating.
- Clear the work area of rocks and debris before starting.
- Power off and remove the battery before clearing jams.

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 2-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
2-year warranty on the motor; battery warranty is 1 year separately.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Clean soil and plant debris from the tines after every use.
- Store the battery indoors in moderate temperatures.
- Sharpen or replace tines annually for best performance.
