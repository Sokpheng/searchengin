GuardianEye Security Camera - User Manual
Model: SC-360
Category: Smart Home
MSRP: $99

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 1080p HD video
- night vision up to 30 feet
- two-way audio
- local and cloud storage options

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Download the companion app and create an account before unboxing.
2. Mount the camera bracket using the included screws and anchors.
3. Power on and scan the QR code on the camera base with the app.
4. Select local (microSD) or cloud storage in the app settings.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E1: Camera offline; check Wi-Fi signal strength at the mounting location.
- E3: microSD card not detected or improperly formatted.
- E6: Motion detection sensitivity misconfigured, causing frequent false alerts.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (SC-360) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Do not mount facing directly into strong sunlight; it can wash out footage.
- Ensure mounting hardware is rated for outdoor use if installing outside.
- Review local laws regarding audio recording before enabling two-way audio.

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 2-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
2-year warranty on hardware; cloud storage subscription is billed separately.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Wipe the lens with a microfiber cloth monthly.
- Check mounting screws for looseness every few months.
- Reformat the microSD card every 6 months to maintain write speed.
