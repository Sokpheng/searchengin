HomeSense Smart Thermostat - User Manual
Model: ST-900
Category: Smart Home
MSRP: $149

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- Wi-Fi enabled
- learns schedule after 1 week
- compatible with most 24V HVAC systems
- geofencing support

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Turn off power to your HVAC system at the breaker before installation.
2. Label existing wires before disconnecting your old thermostat.
3. Mount the new base plate and connect wires per the included wiring guide.
4. Restore power and follow the app's setup wizard to connect Wi-Fi.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E1: Wiring configuration not recognized; verify against the wiring guide.
- E2: Wi-Fi connection lost; check router placement and signal.
- E4: HVAC system not responding; check breaker and wire connections.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (ST-900) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Turn off power at the breaker before any wiring work.
- Do not install near direct sunlight or heat vents, which skews readings.
- Consult an HVAC professional if your system uses a C-wire alternative.

5. WARRANTY

6. MAINTENANCE
- Clean the display with a dry microfiber cloth.
- Update firmware when prompted in the app.
- Replace HVAC filters per your system's schedule for accurate readings.
