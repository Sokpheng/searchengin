ImpactForce Driver - User Manual
Model: ID-450
Category: Power Tools
MSRP: $119

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 18V battery platform
- up to 1800 in-lbs torque
- 3-speed settings
- 1/4-inch hex chuck

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Charge the included battery for at least 60 minutes before first use.
2. Slide the battery into the driver base until it clicks.
3. Select a speed setting appropriate for the fastener size.
4. Insert the driver bit fully into the quick-release chuck.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E2: Battery contacts dirty; clean with a dry cloth.
- E3: Overload detected; ease up on applied pressure.
- E5: Chuck jammed; remove bit and inspect for debris.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (ID-450) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Wear hearing protection; impact mechanism is loud under load.
- Do not use damaged or worn driver bits.
- Keep the tool pointed away from yourself and others when testing the trigger.

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 3-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
3-year warranty matching the PowerDrive drill's battery platform.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Wipe down the chuck weekly to prevent bit slippage.
- Store in the provided case to protect against dust.
- Check battery contacts for corrosion every few months.
