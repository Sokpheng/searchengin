IronGrip Adjustable Dumbbell Set - User Manual
Model: DB-552
Category: Fitness
MSRP: $299

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 5-52.5 lb adjustable range per dumbbell
- quick-turn dial selector
- compact storage tray included
- sold as a pair

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Place both dumbbells in the storage tray before adjusting weight.
2. Turn the selector dial to your desired weight increment.
3. Lift straight up and out of the tray to engage only the selected plates.
4. Return to the tray fully before changing the weight setting.

3. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Always adjust weight with the dumbbell resting in the tray, not in hand.
- Keep fingers clear of the plate selection mechanism.
- Use on a flat, stable surface to prevent tipping.

4. WARRANTY

2-year warranty against mechanical selector failure.

5. MAINTENANCE
- Wipe down the plates after sweaty sessions to prevent rust.
- Check the dial mechanism periodically for smooth operation.
- Store in the tray, not stacked loosely, to protect the selector pins.
