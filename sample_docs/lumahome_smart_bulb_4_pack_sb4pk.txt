LumaHome Smart Bulb 4-Pack - User Manual
Model: SB-4PK
Category: Smart Home
MSRP: $44

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 800 lumens per bulb
- 16 million colors
- Wi-Fi direct, no hub required
- voice assistant compatible

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Screw bulbs into standard E26 sockets and power on.
2. Open the companion app and select 'Add Device'.
3. Follow the in-app prompts to connect each bulb to Wi-Fi individually.
4. Group bulbs into rooms within the app for scene control.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E1: Bulb not discovered during setup; move closer to the router and retry.
- E2: Bulb flickering; check for a compatible dimmer switch (standard dimmers are not supported).
- E5: Voice assistant link expired; re-link the skill/action in its app.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (SB-4PK) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Do not use with incompatible mechanical dimmer switches.
- Do not exceed the fixture's rated wattage equivalent.
- Allow the bulb to cool before handling after extended use.

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 1-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
1-year warranty; excludes bulbs used with unsupported dimmer switches.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Dust the bulb surface occasionally to maintain brightness.
- Keep firmware updated via the app for new features.
- Avoid frequent power cycling, which can shorten bulb lifespan.
