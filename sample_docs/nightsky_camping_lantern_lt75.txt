NightSky Camping Lantern - User Manual
Model: LT-75
Category: Outdoor
MSRP: $29

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 300-lumen LED
- USB-C rechargeable, 20-hour runtime
- IPX6 water resistance
- collapsible design

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Charge fully via USB-C before first use (about 3 hours).
2. Press and hold the power button for 2 seconds to turn on.
3. Cycle through brightness levels with a single press.
4. Collapse the lantern body for compact storage when not lighting an area.

3. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Do not look directly into the LED at close range on high brightness.
- Do not charge while wet; dry the USB-C port fully first.
- Avoid prolonged storage at full charge; store around 50% for battery health.

4. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 1-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
1-year warranty; battery capacity loss after heavy use is not covered.

5. MAINTENANCE
- Wipe the housing with a damp cloth after dusty trips.
- Recharge every few months during long storage periods.
- Check the USB-C port cover seal periodically.
