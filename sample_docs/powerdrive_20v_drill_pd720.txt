PowerDrive 20V Drill - User Manual
Model: PD-720
Category: Power Tools
MSRP: $159

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 20V lithium-ion battery
- two-speed gearbox (0-500 / 0-1800 RPM)
- 1/2-inch keyless chuck
- LED work light

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Charge the battery fully (approx. 90 minutes) before first use.
2. Insert the battery into the drill until it clicks into place.
3. Select forward or reverse rotation with the direction switch.
4. Adjust the clutch collar to the appropriate torque setting for your material.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E1: Battery not fully seated in the drill housing.
- E2: Battery below 10% charge; low-power mode engaged.
- E5: Motor overheated; release the trigger and let cool for 10 minutes.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (PD-720) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Always wear eye protection when drilling.
- Disconnect the battery before changing bits.
- Do not use in wet conditions unless rated for it.

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 3-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
3-year tool warranty; battery warranty is 2 years.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Clean dust from the chuck jaws after each use.
- Store the battery at partial charge if unused for over a month.
- Lubricate the chuck mechanism every 6 months.
