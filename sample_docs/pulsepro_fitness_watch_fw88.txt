PulsePro Fitness Watch - User Manual
Model: FW-88
Category: Electronics
MSRP: $199

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 1.4-inch AMOLED display
- 7-day battery life
- 5 ATM water resistance
- built-in GPS

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Charge fully using the included magnetic charging cable before first use.
2. Download the companion app and create an account.
3. Pair the watch via Bluetooth from the app's device setup screen.
4. Set your profile (height, weight, age) for accurate calorie tracking.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E01: Bluetooth pairing timeout; restart both devices and retry.
- E04: GPS signal not found; move to an open outdoor area.
- E09: Battery critically low; connect the charging cable.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (FW-88) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Not rated for high-velocity water activities like water skiing.
- Avoid charging in temperatures above 95°F.
- Consult a doctor before relying on heart-rate alerts for medical decisions.

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 1-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
1-year limited warranty; battery capacity loss after 12 months is not covered.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Clean the sensor contacts on the back weekly.
- Update firmware monthly via the companion app.
- Avoid prolonged direct sunlight exposure to prevent display degradation.
