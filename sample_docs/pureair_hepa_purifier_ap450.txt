PureAir HEPA Purifier - User Manual
Model: AP-450
Category: Home
MSRP: $159

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- covers up to 450 sq ft
- true HEPA H13 filter
- 3 fan speeds plus auto mode
- air quality sensor

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Remove the plastic wrap from the filter before first use.
2. Place on a flat surface with at least 12 inches of clearance on all sides.
3. Plug in and press the power button; auto mode is recommended for daily use.
4. Check the filter indicator light monthly.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E1: Filter not installed correctly; the unit will not power on.
- E2: Filter replacement indicator active; filter needs replacing.
- E3: Air quality sensor blocked; clean the sensor intake.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (AP-450) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Do not operate without a filter installed.
- Keep away from water sources; this is not a waterproof appliance.
- Do not block the air intake or outlet grilles.

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 2-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
2-year warranty on the motor; filters are consumable and not covered.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Replace the HEPA filter every 6-8 months under normal use.
- Vacuum the pre-filter screen every 2 weeks.
- Wipe the air quality sensor with a dry cotton swab monthly.
