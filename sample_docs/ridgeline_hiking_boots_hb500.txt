RidgeLine Hiking Boots - User Manual
Model: HB-500
Category: Outdoor
MSRP: $139

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- waterproof leather upper
- Vibram outsole
- mid-ankle support
- available in sizes 7-13

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Break in the boots on short walks before long hikes.
2. Use the included insoles or swap for orthotics if needed.
3. Lace snugly at the ankle for support on uneven terrain.
4. Apply waterproofing treatment before first extended use.

3. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Not rated for technical mountaineering or ice climbing.
- Allow boots to fully dry between uses to prevent material breakdown.
- Replace laces if fraying is visible to avoid mid-hike failure.

4. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 1-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
1-year warranty against sole separation and stitching defects.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

5. MAINTENANCE
- Clean mud off after each hike with a soft brush.
- Re-apply waterproofing spray every 2-3 months of regular use.
- Store away from direct heat, which can crack the leather.
