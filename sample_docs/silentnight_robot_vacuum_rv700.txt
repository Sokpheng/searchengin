SilentNight Robot Vacuum - User Manual
Model: RV-700
Category: Home
MSRP: $299

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- LiDAR mapping navigation
- 2700Pa suction
- self-emptying base
- runs up to 150 minutes per charge

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Place the charging base against a wall with 1.5 feet of clearance on each side.
2. Let the robot fully charge before its first cleaning run.
3. Use the app to run an initial mapping pass of your home.
4. Set up no-go zones in the app for areas to avoid.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E1: Wheels stuck or lifted off the ground; place robot on a flat surface.
- E2: Dustbin full; empty it or return to base for auto-empty.
- E5: Side brush tangled with hair or debris; clean and reinstall.
- E9: LiDAR sensor obstructed; wipe the sensor window.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (RV-700) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Keep cords and small objects off the floor to prevent tangling.
- Do not use on wet floors or to pick up liquids.
- Supervise use around stairs even though a drop sensor is included.

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 1-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
1-year warranty; brushes and filters are consumable parts not covered.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Empty and rinse the dustbin weekly.
- Clean the side brushes and rollers of hair every 1-2 weeks.
- Replace the HEPA filter every 3 months.
