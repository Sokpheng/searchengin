SteadyCut Circular Saw - User Manual
Model: CS-185
Category: Power Tools
MSRP: $139

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 7-1/4 inch blade
- 15A corded motor
- bevel cuts up to 56 degrees
- laser guide

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Inspect the blade guard for free movement before use.
2. Set the cutting depth to roughly 1/4 inch deeper than material thickness.
3. Align the laser guide with your cut line.
4. Allow the blade to reach full speed before contacting material.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E1: Blade guard obstructed; will not retract.
- E4: Motor brush wear detected; service recommended.
- E6: Laser guide battery low.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (CS-185) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Never remove or wedge open the blade guard.
- Keep hands at least 6 inches from the blade path.
- Disconnect power before changing blades.

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 2-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
2-year warranty; does not cover blade wear or laser calibration.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Replace the blade after roughly 500 linear feet of cutting.
- Check the bevel adjustment lock monthly.
- Clear sawdust from vents after every use.
