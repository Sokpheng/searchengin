SummitPeak 2-Person Tent - User Manual
Model: TN-220
Category: Outdoor
MSRP: $159

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 2-person capacity
- 3-season rating
- 5.5 lb packed weight
- freestanding aluminum poles

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Lay out the footprint before assembling poles.
2. Assemble the two aluminum poles and cross them at the center.
3. Clip the tent body to the poles, starting from the corners.
4. Stake out the rainfly and guy lines for wind stability.

3. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Do not use as the sole shelter in severe weather; it is rated 3-season only.
- Avoid pitching under dead trees or branches.
- Seal seams before first use in wet conditions.

4. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 2-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
2-year warranty against pole breakage and fabric defects.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

5. MAINTENANCE
- Dry the tent completely before packing away.
- Store loosely rather than tightly compressed long-term.
- Re-seal seams every 1-2 seasons of heavy use.
