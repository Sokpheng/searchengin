TrailBlaze 65L Backpack - User Manual
Model: BP-65
Category: Outdoor
MSRP: $189

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 65-liter capacity
- adjustable torso length 16-21 inches
- aluminum stay frame
- rain cover included

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Adjust the torso length using the back panel slider before loading.
2. Loosen all straps before putting the pack on for the first time.
3. Load heavy items close to your back, roughly at shoulder height.
4. Tighten the hip belt first, then shoulder straps, then load lifters.

3. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Do not exceed the recommended max load of 50 lbs for the frame.
- Check zipper stitching before multi-day trips.
- Store dry to prevent mildew on the fabric.

4. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 5-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
Lifetime warranty against manufacturing defects; normal wear is not covered.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

5. MAINTENANCE
- Hand wash with a mild detergent; never machine wash.
- Air dry completely before storing to prevent mold.
- Re-waterproof the fabric annually with a spray-on treatment.
