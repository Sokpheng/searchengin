TurboBlend Pro Blender - User Manual
Model: BL-500
Category: Kitchen
MSRP: $129

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 1200W motor
- 64oz BPA-free jar
- 6 pre-set programs
- variable speed dial 1-10

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Wash the jar, lid, and blade assembly before first use.
2. Attach the jar to the base and twist until it locks.
3. Select a pre-set program or use the manual speed dial.
4. Always start on the lowest speed when blending hot liquids.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E1: Jar not locked onto the base.
- E2: Motor overload; reduce ingredient volume and retry.
- E6: Lid safety sensor not engaged.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (BL-500) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Never insert utensils into the jar while the motor is running.
- Do not blend liquids hotter than 104°F without venting the lid.
- Keep hands clear of the blade assembly during cleaning.

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 3-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
Motor warranty is 5 years; jar and blades covered for 1 year.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Run a cleaning cycle with warm water and a drop of dish soap after each use.
- Check the blade gasket for wear every 6 months.
- Do not put the base in the dishwasher.
