VoltGuard Power Bank - User Manual
Model: PB-20K
Category: Electronics
MSRP: $39

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 20,000mAh capacity
- 65W USB-C PD fast charging
- 3 output ports
- LED charge indicator

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Charge the power bank fully before first use (roughly 2.5 hours at 65W).
2. Connect your device using any of the 3 output ports.
3. Press the button once to check the remaining charge level.
4. Use the USB-C PD port for fastest charging of compatible laptops.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E1: Overcurrent protection triggered; disconnect and reconnect the cable.
- E2: Internal temperature too high; allow the unit to cool before continuing.
- E3: Short circuit detected on an output port; try a different cable.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (PB-20K) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Do not expose to temperatures above 113°F.
- Do not puncture or disassemble the battery housing.
- Stop use immediately if the case becomes swollen or discolored.

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 2-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
2-year warranty; capacity is expected to degrade to ~80% after 500 charge cycles.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Recharge at least once every 3 months if stored long-term.
- Avoid letting the battery fully drain repeatedly.
- Keep charging ports free of lint and debris.
