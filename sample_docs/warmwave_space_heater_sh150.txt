WarmWave Space Heater - User Manual
Model: SH-150
Category: Home
MSRP: $59

1. KEY SPECIFICATIONS
- 1500W ceramic heating element
- tip-over auto shutoff
- overheat protection
- covers up to 200 sq ft

2. SETUP AND FIRST USE
1. Place on a flat, stable surface away from furniture and curtains.
2. Plug directly into a wall outlet; avoid extension cords if possible.
3. Select the desired heat level and set the thermostat dial.
4. Allow the unit to cycle off automatically once the room reaches target temperature.

3. TROUBLESHOOTING
If an error code appears, refer to the table below:
- E1: Tip-over switch activated; the unit shut off because it was knocked over.
- E2: Overheat protection triggered; unplug and allow 20 minutes to cool.
- E3: Thermostat sensor fault; contact support if error persists.
If the issue persists after following these steps, contact customer support with your model number (SH-150) and a description of the problem.

4. SAFETY WARNINGS
- Keep at least 3 feet from bedding, curtains, and furniture.
- Never leave unattended while children or pets are nearby.
- Do not use in bathrooms or other damp areas unless rated for it (this model is not).

5. WARRANTY
This product is covered by a 2-year limited warranty from the date of purchase, covering defects in materials and workmanship under normal use.
2-year warranty on the heating element and housing.
Proof of purchase is required for all warranty claims.

6. MAINTENANCE
- Dust the intake and outlet grilles every 2 weeks during heating season.
- Inspect the power cord for fraying before each season of use.
- Store in a dry location during the off-season.
